About ODU Collector

Version: 2.0.0

Project: Airtel Zambia 5G WiFi Router IMEI Collection

This application is designed to streamline the collection and installation tracking of ODU (Outdoor Unit) 5G WiFi routers. Field installers can scan or manually enter IMEI numbers, which are then synced in real-time to Firestore.

Supervisors have an overarching view of all devices collected within their assigned cluster, allowing for seamless monitoring of installation progress.
